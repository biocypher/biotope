# Notes on selected US airports

A short reference describing airline hubs at a handful of major US
airports. Each entry uses the IATA code as a heading so it's easy to
match against the structured airport records.

## ATL

Hartsfield–Jackson Atlanta International. The world's busiest airport
by passenger traffic for most of the past two decades. Delta Air Lines'
primary hub.

## DFW

Dallas/Fort Worth International. American Airlines' largest hub and one
of its principal connecting points.

## JFK

John F. Kennedy International, in Queens, New York. A major hub for
Delta, American, and JetBlue.

## LAX

Los Angeles International. A hub for American, Delta, and United.

## ORD

Chicago O'Hare International. A major hub for both United and American.

## SFO

San Francisco International. United Airlines' primary trans-Pacific
hub; also served as an Alaska Airlines focus city.
